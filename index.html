I am a scientist affiliated to KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My focus, in its essence, is on complex dynamical systems and its manifestations. I leverage the power of data and computing to understand, visualise and tinker with these systems. My primary intention in setting up this site is to organize my research and report my findings. My secondary intention is pedagogical. Science can be labyrinthine and intimidating. In the information rich world we live in, there is an admixture of the real with the spurious making the discernment of right from wrong difficult. It is essential to communicate the complexities and intricacies of ideas without sacrificing scientific integrity. The essays/articles below are geared towards such an aim. I also write on topics beyond my research areas (organized under science, society and culture). For a detailed expansion of my personal philosophy, read my manifesto. My CV contains my academic credentials, project and publication list. Technical reports and articles are uploaded to RG and arXiv.
I write fiction and make art. Some autobiographical ruminations are collected here. I also have a site dedicated to Indian Philosophy with a marked focus on Dvaita. I occasionally publish articles on Academia.edu. Technical reports, articles and posters are uploaded to RG and arXiv. I blog about a variety of topics on Medium.
