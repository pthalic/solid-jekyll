I am an independent researcher affiliated to KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My focus, in its essence, is on complex dynamical systems and its manifestations. I leverage the power of data and computing to understand, visualise and tinker with these systems. My primary intention in setting up this site is to organize my research and report my findings. My secondary intention is pedagogical. Science can be labyrinthine and intimidating. In the information rich world we live in, there is an admixture of the real with the spurious making the discernment of right from wrong difficult. It is essential to communicate the complexities and intricacies of ideas without sacrificing scientific integrity. The essays/articles below are geared towards such an aim. The articles pertaining to the research areas that are of interest to me are organized under brain, machine learning and power). For a discussion regarding my philosophy when it comes to research, read my manifesto. My CV contains my academic credentials, project and publication list. Technical reports and articles are uploaded to RG and arXiv.
